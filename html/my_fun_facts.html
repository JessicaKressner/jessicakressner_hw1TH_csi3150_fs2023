My Favorite Book

My Favorite book is Legend by Marie Lu


Buy Legend

Legend by Marie Lu is my favorite book because it is a distopian fiction that tells a story of two people from different social classes and ways of life and their worlds collide when they run into each other. As they get to know each other they discover the secrets of the government and how corrupt the society they live in is. There are many plot twists that will keep your interest.
